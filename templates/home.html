How to Workout

In order to make the most out of your workout with AI Gym Tracker, make sure you have enough space to move around and position yourself in front of your webcam so that your entire body is visible to the AI. From there, simply perform your preferred exercises and let our system track your repetitions and timing. Once your session is complete, receive detailed training statistics to help you measure your progress and stay motivated.
